Choose Your Nature Element

A shinobi's chakra nature defines their affinity for Jutsus. In this Dojo, your nature is locked upon registration. You can only perform the secret art of your chosen element.

WIND AFFINITY 風

Unlocks the Rasengan. Condense wind chakra by forming a 🤙 Shaka Sign (extend your Thumb & Pinky, curl other fingers).

LIGHTNING AFFINITY 雷

Unlocks the Chidori. Gather electricity by forming a 🤘 Rock-on Sign (extend your Index & Pinky, curl middle/ring fingers).

FIRE AFFINITY 火

Unlocks the Fireball Jutsu. Expel flames by holding a 🖐️ Open Flat Palm facing the camera.

SHADOW AFFINITY 影

Unlocks the Shadow Clone Jutsu. Form clones using the Confrontation seal: make a ✌️ Peace Sign (extend Index & Middle fingers).

Rules of the Dojo

Follow these instructions to complete your training and rank up:

  • Locked Affinity: Once you register, you cannot change your nature type during the training session. Make your choice wisely.
  • Jutsu Sustainment: Form and hold the required hand sign for a moment. Once invoked, the Jutsu is locked active! You can release the sign, move your hands, and run around while the Jutsu remains active, just like in the anime.
  • Chakra Exhaustion: Casting Jutsus consumes your chakra. If your chakra drops to 0%, you enter a state of exhaustion.
  • Gather Sparks Mini-game: To recover your chakra, you must touch the glowing chakra spark targets that appear on the screen using your hand. Collecting all sparks restores your chakra.
  • Ninja Ranks & Infinite Use: Earn XP by casting Jutsus and gathering chakra. Rank up from Academy Student, Genin, Chunin, to Jonin. Reaching the legendary rank of Kage grants you infinite chakra with 0% cost!
